About the Camp

Overview

The Royal Rangers Zonal Camping Program is an annual gathering of Rangers from across the zone, designed to foster fellowship, leadership development, spiritual growth, and practical skills training in an outdoor setting.

Young Rangers and experienced leaders come together for a structured program of activities, worship, drills, and competitions that build character, discipline, and lasting bonds of brotherhood and sisterhood.

Camp Objectives

  • Develop leadership skills and self-discipline among young Rangers
  • Strengthen spiritual foundation through Bible study and worship
  • Build teamwork, unity, and camaraderie across outposts and districts
  • Train in practical life skills: first aid, navigation, and survival techniques
  • Celebrate achievements and recognize outstanding Rangers
  • Promote excellence in Royal Rangers values and mission

Camp Rules & Regulations

  • All participants must wear full uniform during official camp activities
  • Lights out at designated time; wake-up as per daily schedule
  • Mobile phones restricted during all scheduled program activities
  • No unauthorized movement outside designated camp zones
  • Respect all officials, instructors, and fellow campers at all times
  • All valuables are the personal responsibility of the individual camper
  • Medical emergencies must be reported to the medical team immediately
  • Participation in all devotional and spiritual activities is compulsory
